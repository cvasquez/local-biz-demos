About Perfect Degree

The team behind Drexel Hill's most trusted HVAC company

At Perfect Degree HVAC, we believe every home deserves reliable comfort — no matter the season. Based right here in Drexel Hill, we've built our reputation on honest work, fair pricing, and the kind of personal service that only a local company can provide.

When your furnace goes out on the coldest night of the year or your AC struggles through a July heatwave, you want a team you can count on. That's us. We show up on time, explain what's happening in plain language, and get the job done right the first time.

Our customers are our neighbors — families in Drexel Hill, Havertown, Ardmore, and throughout Delaware County. Many of them have trusted us with their homes for years, and we take that responsibility seriously.

With a perfect 5.0-star rating from 259 Google reviews, we let our work speak for itself. Every review represents a family we've helped stay comfortable, and every five-star rating reminds us why we do what we do.
