Our Story

About Us

Two friends, a shared love of flowers, and a backyard that needed a purpose.

From Our Garden to Yours

Two Budding Florists started the way the best things do — with dirt under our fingernails and a wild idea. We're two friends from Havertown who fell in love with growing flowers and decided to share them with our community.

What began as a few rows of zinnias and sunflowers in a backyard plot has grown into a small but mighty flower operation. We grow seasonally, using sustainable practices, and we design every arrangement by hand with whatever's blooming best.

You'll find us most weekends at the Oakmont Farmers Market with bunches of fresh-cuts, or you can order for local delivery anywhere in Havertown, Drexel Hill, and the surrounding Delaware County area.

  • Grown locally — never shipped from overseas
  • Sustainable, no-spray growing practices
  • Every arrangement is one-of-a-kind
  • Committed to our Havertown community

What Drives Us

Grow With Intention

We choose every variety with care — for beauty, for fragrance, and for how well it thrives in our local soil and climate.

Sustainability First

No pesticides, minimal waste, compostable wrapping. We believe beautiful flowers shouldn't come at the earth's expense.

Community Roots

Havertown is our home. We love connecting with our neighbors at the farmers market and through local deliveries.

Every Stem Counts

We put thought and care into every arrangement. No filler, no fluff — just honest, beautiful flowers.
