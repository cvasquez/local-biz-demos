How We Can Help You Bloom

Custom Bouquets

Tell us the occasion and we'll create something beautiful from whatever's freshest in our garden that week.

  • Seasonal mixed bouquets
  • Single-variety bunches
  • Color-coordinated arrangements
  • Gift wrapping included
  • Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, thank-yous

Flower Subscriptions

Fresh flowers on your schedule. We'll deliver a seasonal arrangement right to your door — no two are alike.

  • Weekly, biweekly, or monthly options
  • Delivered to your door in Havertown area
  • Seasonal varieties that change throughout the year
  • Great as a gift subscription
  • Pause or cancel anytime

Event Flowers

Planning a party, shower, or small gathering? We'll create arrangements that set the mood perfectly.

  • Bridal & bridal shower flowers
  • Corsages & boutonnieres
  • Ceremony & reception florals
  • Consultation to match your vision

Farmers Market

Come see us at the Oakmont Farmers Market for grab-and-go bunches, potted herbs, and seasonal specialties.

  • Fresh-cut bunches ready to take home
  • Potted plants & herbs
  • Dried flower arrangements (fall/winter)
  • Seasonal wreaths & garlands
  • Chat with us about custom orders

Local Delivery
