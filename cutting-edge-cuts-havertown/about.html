Our Story

About Cutting Edge Cuts

More than a barbershop — we're part of the Havertown community.

Havertown's Go-To Barbershop

Cutting Edge Cuts has been a fixture on Eagle Road in Havertown for years. We started with a simple idea: give every customer a great haircut at a fair price in a shop that feels like home. That mission hasn't changed.

Walk into our shop on any given day and you'll find neighbors catching up, kids getting their first haircut, and regulars who've been coming to us for years. That's the kind of barbershop we set out to build — and that's what keeps us going.

We're proud to serve Havertown and the surrounding Delaware County communities. Whether you live around the corner or you're just passing through, you're always welcome at Cutting Edge Cuts.

What We Stand For

Community First

We're invested in Havertown. Our customers are our neighbors, and we treat every person who walks through the door like family.

Quality Over Shortcuts

We take our time with every cut. No rushing, no assembly-line approach — just careful, skilled barbering.

All Are Welcome

Men, women, kids — if you need a great haircut, we're your shop. We work with all hair types and styles.

Passion for the Craft

Barbering is more than a job for us. We genuinely love what we do, and it shows in every cut.
